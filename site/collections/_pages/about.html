I’m a software developer in the Louisville, KY area. I am an Ubuntu member, an Xfce contributor, and the Xubuntu Technical Lead. The applications I develop are powered by C, Python, Vala, and GTK to be lightweight, fast, and efficient.

I received my degree in Computer Science from Berea College. Since then, I worked in QA for a couple of years before finding my calling in web and app development. I’m now working as a Software Development Manager in Louisville, KY.
